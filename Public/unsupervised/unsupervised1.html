Unsupervised Learning

Unlike supervised learning, unsupervised learning works with data that has NO labels or correct answers. The AI must find patterns, groups, or structures on its own.

üîÅ The key difference: No labels, no "correct" answers to compare against

Common tasks in unsupervised learning:

  • Clustering: Grouping similar items together
  • Pattern Recognition: Finding hidden patterns in data
  • Dimensionality Reduction: Simplifying complex data

Example: If you show an AI 1000 photos with NO labels, it might discover on its own that some photos are of cats, some are dogs, and some are birds - just by finding patterns in the pixels.
