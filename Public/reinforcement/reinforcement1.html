Reinforcement Learning

Reinforcement learning is when an AI learns through trial and error, receiving rewards for good actions and penalties for bad actions.

üîÅ The reinforcement learning loop:

  1. The AI takes an action in an environment
  2. The environment gives feedback (reward or penalty)
  3. The AI learns which actions lead to rewards
  4. The AI adjusts its strategy to maximize rewards

Think of it like training a dog - you give treats (rewards) for good behavior and ignore or correct (penalties) for bad behavior. Over time, the dog learns what actions get rewards.
